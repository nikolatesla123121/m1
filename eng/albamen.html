Turkey's First Children's Space Encyclopedia

Embark on a journey into the depths of space with Albamen and Lara. Stories of Turkish astronauts, Mars missions and the secrets of the universe await in this book.

This book is crafted with cutting-edge technology for future scientists, STEM learners and autodidacts — Turkey's first super-hero for young readers. Presented by Albamen, this children's space encyclopedia includes augmented reality, AI and QR-code enabled features starring our hero astronauts Alper Gezeravcı and Tuva Cihangir. It contains many topics such as Atasever's experiments in space, spacecraft, Mars missions, and notable Turkish scientists. Before you start reading as a future scientist or astronaut, have your smartphone or tablet ready to see spacecraft up close. Come on, space-friend, buckle up—the space journey begins!
