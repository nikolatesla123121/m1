ALBA SPACE

A space initiative making space, science, and technology accessible to everyone.

Founded in 2022 in Turkey, ALBA SPACE is the first and only space-technology initiative in the country that sends brands to space and conducts scientific experiments in the stratosphere. With its professional and specialised team, Alba Space plays an important role in shaping the future in a positive way through social responsibility and awareness projects in the field of space technologies, prioritising the health of future generations and our planet based on a carbon-neutral principle.
